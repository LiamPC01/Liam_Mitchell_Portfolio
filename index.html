Space Cat Nip

Space Cat Nip was created by a team of three (two programmers and an artist) over a weekend for the 2022 Global Game Jam.
It is a third person action platformer where the player must navigate to the end of each level to
retrieve their lost cat nip whilst leaping accross dangerous low gravity jumps and shooting angry robots.
I programmed and designed the levels.

Hardest Route

Hardest Route was created over a weekend for the 2023 Global Game Jam.
Our team was made up of two programmers, two asset artists and one concept artist.
Hardest Route is a Dungeon Crawler where the player must try to take as much damage as possible
before the timer runs out. They can achieve this by taking the route through the level with
the most enemies.
Shown below in the NUA Global Game Jam showcase reel as the first video.

Space Cat Nip

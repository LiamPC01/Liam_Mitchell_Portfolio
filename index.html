Hardest Root

Hardest Root was created over a weekend for the 2023 Global Game Jam.
Our team was made up of two programmers, two asset artists and one concept artist.
Hardest Root is a Dungeon Crawler where the player must try to take as much damage as possible
before the timer runs out. They can achieve this by taking the route through the level with
the most enemies.
Shown below in the NUA Global Game Jam showcase reel as the first video.

Space Cat Nip

Space Cat Nip was created in Unreal by a team of three,
two programmers and one artist, over a weekend for the 2022 Global Game Jam.
It is a third person action platformer where the player must reach the end of each
level to retrieve their lost cat nip whilst leaping accross dangerous low gravity jumps
and shooting angry robots. I programmed using C++ and designed the levels.

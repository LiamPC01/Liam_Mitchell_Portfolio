Landing Page

Project Objective

Create a professional webpage based on a design document, utilizing HTML, CSS, and Flexbox for structure, styling, and layout.

The Process

  • Constructed the webpage structure using HTML.
  • Styled the webpage with CSS to meet design document specifications.
  • Employed Flexbox to achieve a clean, professional layout.
  • Used Flexbox to group elements and control their presentation, including vertical and horizontal alignment, as well as specific positioning.

The Outcome

  • Delivered a well-organized and visually appealing webpage that aligns with the design document.
  • Demonstrated proficiency in HTML, CSS, and Flexbox, enhancing layout management and overall design quality.
